About Kids Planet Uganda

Our Story: A Cambridge Preschool in Kawempe

Kids Planet Uganda is a trusted nursery school in Kawempe, Kampala. Our story began with one simple belief: every child belongs. Parents choose us because we combine a Cambridge preschool education with Christian values, small class sizes, and inclusive support for all learners.

Located in Kawempe Lugoba, our preschool and daycare centre serves families who want a safe, nurturing learning environment for their children. We are proud to welcome mainstream learners, baby school students, and children with special learning needs. At Kids Planet, each child is guided with patience, care, and understanding.

Children learn differently, and every journey matters. That is why we create a warm community where children can explore, ask questions, and grow with confidence. Our teachers are trained to recognize each child’s strengths and support their social and emotional development.
