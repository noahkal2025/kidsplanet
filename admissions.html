How to Apply

Joining Kids Planet Uganda begins with a conversation. Call or WhatsApp our admissions office at 0740941232, and we will arrange a school visit in Kawempe. During your visit, you can meet teachers, tour classrooms, and learn how our programs support both mainstream learners and children with special learning needs.

To secure a place, complete our application form, provide your child’s birth certificate, immunization records, and any relevant reports. We welcome families from all faiths and backgrounds, and we are happy to support parents throughout the process.

Step 1: Schedule a School Visit

See our classrooms, meet our caring teachers, and feel the warm atmosphere that makes Kids Planet Uganda special.

Step 2: Complete the Application

Fill out our admissions form and share details about your child’s interests, strengths, and support needs.

Step 3: Submit Documents

Provide birth certificate, immunization records, and school reports, if available.

Step 4: Join Our Community

Receive confirmation and guidance for your child’s first day at our nursery school in Kawempe.

What to Expect

Our admissions process is personal and thoughtful. We do not rush families. We listen to your hopes for your child and explain how our Cambridge preschool program supports early literacy, numeracy, and creative play.

We also explain how our inclusive preschool Uganda approach supports children with special learning needs. That means you can ask about individual learning plans, classroom adaptations, and how teachers work together with parents.

Kids Planet Uganda is more than a daycare centre in Kampala. It is a place where faith, curiosity, and kindness grow together, and where each child finds a safe place to belong.
