Inclusive Learning

Inclusive Preschool for Mainstream Learners and Children with Special Learning Needs

At Kids Planet Uganda, we believe every child belongs. Our inclusive preschool in Kampala welcomes mainstream learners and children with special learning needs in a supportive and caring community.

Our approach is gentle, individualized, and built around the idea that children learn differently. We celebrate those differences and help every learner thrive.

We are proud to serve families from Kawempe Lugoba and beyond with a Cambridge preschool program that also provides early intervention and social development support.

A Place Where Every Child Belongs

Individualized Support

Each child receives the care they need through small class sizes and attention from caring teachers.

Early Intervention

We provide early support for children with autism, ADHD, speech delays, and other learning differences.

Social Development

Inclusive activities help children build friendships, confidence, and emotional resilience.

Christian Values

Our school is faith-friendly, with values that encourage kindness, patience, and compassion.

How We Support Children with Special Learning Needs

Our teachers work closely with parents to understand each child’s strengths, challenges, and learning style. We build routines that reduce anxiety and encourage success.

Children with special learning needs are not separated. They learn alongside friends in a classroom where differences are honored and celebrated.

Every child’s progress is guided with patience, care, and understanding. We believe that emotional growth is just as important as academic progress.

We Support Children With

  • Autism spectrum disorders
  • ADHD and attention differences
  • Speech and language challenges
  • Emotional and behavioral needs
  • Developmental delays

Our Inclusive Approach

Small Class Sizes

Small groups allow our teachers to spend quality time with each child and respond to changing needs.

Flexible Learning

Lessons are adapted to include movement, visuals, and hands-on activities so every child can participate.

Safe Environment

Our preschool is a calm, welcoming space where children feel secure and supported.

Parent Partnership

We work with families to share progress, celebrate milestones, and make sure each child feels understood.
